# E-Commerce Marketing Patterns

Specific guidance for online stores and physical product marketing.

## E-Commerce Challenges

- **Can't touch the product**: Must compensate with visuals, descriptions
- **Trust barrier**: Will it arrive? Is it quality?
- **Comparison shopping**: Easy to check competitors
- **Cart abandonment**: ~70% average abandonment rate

### Checkout Flow

**Best practices:**
1. Guest checkout option (don't force account)
2. Progress indicator
3. Minimize form fields
4. Address autocomplete
5. Save payment methods
6. Order summary visible
7. Trust signals throughout

### Abandoned Cart Recovery

**Email sequence:**
1. **1 hour**: "You left something behind" (no discount)
2. **24 hours**: "Still thinking it over?" (social proof)
3. **72 hours**: "Here's 10% off" (discount if needed)

## Conversion Tactics

### Urgency (When Genuine)
- "Only 3 left in stock"
- "Order in 2:14 for same-day shipping"
- "Sale ends Sunday"

### Social Proof
- "47 people viewing this"
- "Bought 12 times today"
- Review highlights

### Bundle & Upsell
- "Frequently bought together"
- "Complete the look"
- "Add matching item - 20% off"

### Exit Intent
- "Don't forget your cart!"
- "Get 10% off your first order"
- Email capture for abandoned browsers
