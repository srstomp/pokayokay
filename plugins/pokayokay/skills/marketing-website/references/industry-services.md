# Services Marketing Patterns

Specific guidance for professional services, agencies, and consulting.

## Services Challenges

- **Intangible offering**: Can't see or test before buying
- **Trust-dependent**: Relies on expertise and relationships
- **High consideration**: Often significant investment
- **Comparison difficulty**: Services vary widely in scope/quality

### Case Study Page Structure
```
1. Hero: Client, outcome headline, key metric
2. Challenge: What problem they faced
3. Solution: What you did (process, approach)
4. Results: Specific, measurable outcomes
5. Testimonial: Quote from client
6. CTA: "Get similar results"
```

## Services Copy Tips

### Emphasize Expertise
- Years of experience
- Industry specializations
- Team credentials
- Client roster

### Focus on Outcomes
- ❌ "We provide marketing services"
- ✅ "We help B2B companies generate qualified leads"

### Reduce Risk
- Free consultation
- No long-term contracts
- Satisfaction guarantees
- Clear process/timeline

### Be Specific
- ❌ "We've helped many clients"
- ✅ "We've helped 200+ companies increase revenue by an average of 47%"
